Meri Kannu 👸

Kannu… main tumhe khona nahi chahta. Ye ek baat hai jo mujhe har waqt satati hai — ki agar kabhi tum mere se door chali gayi toh main kya karunga?

Tumse wada karta hoon — har situation mein, har mushkil mein, har khushi mein — main tumhare saath rahunga. Koi argument ho, koi takrar ho, tum kitna bhi naraz ho mujhse… main wahan rahunga. Baar baar, har baar.

"Tum naraz ho sakti ho mujhse, muh phair sakti ho, magar main jaaunga nahi — kyunki tum meri duniya ho."

Tum roti ho toh mera dil toot jaata hai. Tum hansti ho toh main sab kuch bhool jaata hoon. Tumhari ek chhoti si baat bhi mujhe poora din yaad rehti hai. Ye sab sirf isliye nahi keh main chahta hoon — balki isliye kyunki tum mere liye wakai bahut khaas ho, Kannu.

Jo log sachchi parwah karte hain, woh kabhi chhodte nahi. Main bhi nahi chodhunga. Hamesha manaunga. Hamesha.
